Overview

The SEO Audit Tool by Next Edge Innovations is a web-based application designed to analyze the SEO health of your website. With just a few clicks, it checks crucial SEO elements such as page titles, meta descriptions, heading tags, content length, image alt attributes, internal and external links, and DNS records. The tool generates a detailed report highlighting strengths, weaknesses, and actionable recommendations to enhance your website’s search engine visibility and ranking.

Features

  • Title Tag Analysis: Checks presence and optimal length of page titles.
  • Meta Description Review: Validates meta descriptions for length and relevance
  • Heading Tag Inspection: Identifies and counts H1, H2, and H3 tags, highlighting SEO best practices.

  • Image Alt Text Check: Detects images missing alt attributes. etc
